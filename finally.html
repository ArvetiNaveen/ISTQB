what is techfarming:

                         High Technology Farming is a broad concept which refers to a wide range of new tools (Robotics, ICT, Big Data, Earth Observation, etc.).
The synergic use of these instruments allows the shifting to the new paradigm of Sustainable Precision Agriculture (SPF).

Techno Serve


                     TechnoServe's origin traces back to the village of Adidome, Ghana. They have expanded through the years, but their focus remains 
  on the developing world because that's where they can make the greatest impact on global poverty. They work in poor communities in 29 countries throught
 Africa, Latin America and Asia. Technoserve works with enterprising people in the developing world to build competative forms, business and industries. they help
grow strong markets that create income and jobs in poor communities. In order to achieve their mission. They operate as a catalyst and partner to strenthen market
 systems. They focus on market systems that have :

  • A clear oppurtunity-an unmet demand for product or service
  • The potential for inclusive growth that benefits poor people in their roles as producers,
entrepreneurs, employees or consumers.
  • The potential for scale-impacting significant numbers of families.

Techno Chemicals


      Techno chemicals is a part of renowned RUBAMIN group,engaged in manufacturing of zinc,moly derivatives in india. 
Techno chemicals was established in the year 1989 for manufacturing of cadium oxide on large scale.
Since then the company has established its reputation in the local and overseas market by maintaining strict quality standard of its product and services.
The company has been making strides by continuosly upgrading the quality to fall in line with the changing needs of the consuming industries in the country and abroad.
Today the company is in a position to satisfy the diverse needs of different industries in terms of quality due to its strong infrastructure base.Head office is in mumbai.
